Our Love Our Love Story

  1. Pwede ba manligaw?

    10th of April 2012

    Their courtship stage started when they were in 3rd year high school. As typical high school sweethearts, they were shy, and they seldom talk personally; they enjoy talking via text message exchanging “pick-up lines” that were very famous back then.

  2. Oo, tayo na!

    24th of August 2013

    They became officially in a relationship after 1 year and 4 months of courtship. During their college days, they do not usually see each other because Erika studied in Manila while Arnel studied in Bulacan. However, they did not allow that situation to adversely affect their relationship. In fact, it made their relationship stronger.

  3. Will you marry me?

    3rd of May 2022

    After almost 10 years as boyfriend and girlfriend, Arnel proposed to Erika.

  4. Yes, I do!

    21st of May 2023

    This date marks the beginning of a lifetime with each other.
